About

I specialize in cloud architecture, microservices modernization, distributed systems design, observability, and AI-assisted engineering practices. My work focuses on building scalable, reliable enterprise platforms and modernizing legacy systems in regulated environments.

Alongside product engineering, I actively publish articles on modernization, reliability, AI infrastructure, security, and architecture patterns. My public work spans DZone, Substack, Medium, and peer-reviewed conference research focused on production-ready AI systems, distributed architecture, and secure agentic execution.

My recent research has been accepted for GAISS 2026 and IEEE SmartCloud 2026, strengthening a body of work at the intersection of enterprise AI, distributed systems reliability, and secure token-based agent execution.
