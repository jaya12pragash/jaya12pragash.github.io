About

I specialize in cloud architecture, microservices modernization, distributed systems design, observability, and AI-assisted engineering practices. My work focuses on building scalable, reliable enterprise platforms and modernizing legacy systems in regulated environments.

Alongside product engineering, I publish technical articles and newsletter editions on modernization, reliability, AI infrastructure, security, and architecture patterns. My public work spans DZone, LinkedIn Newsletter, Substack, Medium, book projects, and peer-reviewed research submissions.

My current research focuses on dependency-aware anomaly detection, root cause localization, secure agentic execution, token vault architecture, latency amplification, and production reliability in distributed AI and microservice systems.
